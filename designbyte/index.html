Welcome to designbyte!

This site is intended to work as a clear, concise design guide for anyone to read over and be able to make design decisions. Whether you are a developer or a just looking to learn a bit more about design, designbyte is happy to help!

Topics and terminology

After much consideration, a few number of terms were selected for this site. While there are only a few, the ideas are all important factors in making design choices.

Each page is designed with the content in mind, and features examples and some interactive elements to demonstrate the concepts.

Terms

Accessibility

Accessibility is a principle which relates to the usability of a system for any and all users. Here, we discuss the topics of physical and visual impairments and their relationship with designing systems.
