2001: A Space Odyssey Movie Poster

A project for an Adobe Illustrator class in around 2016 at Cuesta College in San Luis Obispo, California. Per the assignment, a 24 inch by 36 inch poster was to be designed for a movie, using Adobe Illustrator for vector-based type and main elements.

Given that Stanley Kubrick's 2001: A Space Odyssey is among my favorite films, I produced my take on a poster for it. The poster depicts HAL beside David Bowman, before a vibrant background, and below some futuristic-influenced type and the monolith.
