About RaceTrac Communications

At RaceTrac, we're all about ensuring your communication services are fast, reliable, and secure. Our efforts go beyond those of others, as we put time and resources into the things that matter to you.

Speed

Even our most cost effective internet plans are developed with speed in mind. Our infrastructure consists of state of the art, fiber optic, gigabit compatible lines, in an effort to provide the best quality experiences. We can optimize the speeds you need with our different packages!

Reliability

We take the reliability of our services very seriously, and try our hardest to maintain our infrastructure in an effort to minimize down-time. Though disasters cannot be predicted, we can still do our very best to minimize down-time to ensure you have access to your services. Security

As an internet service provider, we have access to a lot of personal information, but at RaceTrac we firmly believe that your data is your data. Despite the controversial repeal of the Net Neutrality Act, we will never sell your data. We refuse to be tempted by the payouts which ad agencies offer to internet service providers, and would much rather see our customers happy and safe.

On top of this, we are committed to ensuring your data is secure in our networks. Our well versed security team does its job to prevent network abuse, infiltration, denial of service attacks, and other cybercrimes.

Customer Service

You are the reason we exist, and for that reason we are committed to making sure you are satisfied with our services. If you are ever in need of assistance, have questions or concerns, or even just want to chat, do contact us!
