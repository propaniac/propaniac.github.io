Nintendo 64 Controller Technical Drawing

A techical drawing style depiction of a Nintendo 64 controller, a somewhat infamous product design that was pivotal in my upbringing and a large stepping stone toward modern gaming as a whole. The drawing depicts a blend between an internal and external view, showing the motherboard and exterior shell of the controller. This drawing was created for a project in an Adobe Illustrator class in around 2016 at Cuesta College in San Luis Obispo, California. A basic video was created with screenshots taken during the creative process.
